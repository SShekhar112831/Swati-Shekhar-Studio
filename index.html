About

An introduction to her journey as an artist and tutor

Swati is a multidisciplinary artist with a deep passion for Indian folk and contemporary art forms such as Madhubani, Warli, Mandala, Zentangle, and Calligraphy. With years of hands-on practice, exhibitions, and workshops, she blends tradition with modern aesthetics.

Beyond art, she offers personalized home tuitions in Hindi and Handwriting for students up to class X. Her sessions are structured, friendly, and outcome-focused, helping students build confidence and clarity.
